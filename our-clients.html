" mw Our Work "

Transforming data collection and analysis with
adaptive tech for development and impact

Microware has, for 20 years now, provided multi thematic solutions in the social sector space. Working across 29 states of India, Microware has delivered off-line, on-line and web based solutions – practical, sustainable and easy to use, across a multitude of clients – including bilateral and multi lateral agencies, Government of India, Departments, State governments, Projects and Programs, Foundations and Trusts, NGOs

and International funding agencies and NGOs.  Our software solutions have won various awards, and there are packages and systems that are proprietary in nature, including GRASP – the Groundwater analysis package, and MicroLekha, which is the complete implementation of the Grameen Foundation implementation of Micro Finance.

01

Agriculture

Microware has engaged with multiple Agri programs, both as part of their livelihood initiatives, and otherwise. The more prominent programs, other than larger livelihood systems, include Reliance BIJ (Bharat India Jodo) across 26 clusters in India, The Axis Bank Foundation efforts across India, ASI Walmart’s interventions in UP and AP, Reviving the Green Revolution of the Tata Trusts in Punjab and Tamil Nadu, and various SRI, market linkage and crop monitoring software for various programmes, including the Mahila Kisan Sashaktiran Pariyojana in Jharkhand .  The software currently in use include generic definitions for crop monitoring, and provide for analysis of yield and price realisation, including monitoring of Producer Groups and co-operatives. Some systems follow the entire crop cycle, and also keep track the payments due to community resources for agriculture and animal husbandry. Systems have been built for animal husbandry, micro enterprises and market linkages.
